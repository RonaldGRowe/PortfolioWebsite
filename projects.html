Streaming Tracker

This was a final project where I was assigned to come up with my own concept. I thought having a way to track what I was watching would be useful. Sometimes you forget what episode you are on, if you watched that movie, or if a show was terrible. I created a user login page so multiple users could utilize the app. After logging in you could choose between TV Shows, Movies, or Shows to Avoid. The most complex part was linking the mutiple form pages together and getting them to hide in the background when accessing different forms.

Tithe Keeper

I was tasked with creating a C# Windows Form that would help a church figure out how their congragation wanted to use their tithes. It was linked to a Microsoft Access database that stored and kept track of all the allocated tithes. I ran into a little trouble getting one of the database statements to accept a variable. The syntax was a little tricky and hard to find in the documentation. The program also created text files to serve as a way to double check when discrepancies appeared.
